## Overview
The Silver Ripple Charm Bracelet features hand-hammered flat oval links that create a soft,
undulating surface across the chain. The subtle texture catches light naturally, producing
a refined ripple effect without sacrificing wearability.

Finished with the **CDLA bronze hallmark charm**, detailed with the signature honeycomb texture,
this piece balances movement, craftsmanship, and everyday versatility.

**Total: $92 USD**
