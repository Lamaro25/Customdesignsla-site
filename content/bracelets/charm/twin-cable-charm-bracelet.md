## Overview
The Twin Cable Charm Bracelet features two parallel sterling-silver cable strands,
joined by dapped links that introduce depth, structure, and added brilliance.
The dual-layer construction gives this bracelet a more substantial presence
while maintaining flexibility and comfort on the wrist.

Finished with the **CDLA bronze hallmark charm** engraved with the signature
honeycomb texture, this piece blends strength, refinement, and modern craftsmanship.

**Total: $123 USD**

## Specifications
- **Material:** Solid .925 Sterling Silver  
- **Accent:** CDLA Bronze Hallmark Charm  
- **Link Style:** Dual cable chain with dapped connectors  
- **Finish:** Polished  
- **Closure:** Lobster clasp  
- **Sizes Available:**  
  - Petite — 6 5/8 in (S)  
  - Standard — 7 1/4 in (M)  
  - Generous — 8 in (L)  
  - Extra — 8 3/8 in (XL)  
- **Crafted:** Hand-assembled in Del Rio, Texas  

## Notes
- Twin-strand design offers added weight and visual depth  
- Dapped links enhance light reflection without sharp edges  
- Balanced profile for daily wear or statement stacking  
- Bronze hallmark charm will develop natural patina over time
