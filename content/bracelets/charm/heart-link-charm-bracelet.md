## Overview
The Heart Link Charm Bracelet brings a softer, expressive edge to everyday sterling silver.
Its open heart-shaped links create a playful rhythm while maintaining a balanced, wearable profile
that layers easily or stands on its own.

Finished with the **CDLA bronze hallmark charm**, featuring the signature honeycomb texture,
this piece carries both sentiment and craftsmanship without feeling overly decorative.

**Total: $87 USD**

- **Accent:** CDLA Bronze Hallmark Charm  
- **Link Style:** Open heart-shaped links  
- **Finish:** Polished  
- **Closure:** Lobster clasp  
- **Sizes Available:**
